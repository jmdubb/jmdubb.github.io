Asist. cercet. dr. Claudia Chiorean

Research assistant Ph.D.

Claudia (Talașman) Chiorean is a Scientific Research Assistant at the Institute for Social Research, Department of Journalism and Digital Media, College of Political Sciences, Babeş-Bolyai University. She graduated in 2002 with a specialization in Journalism and a master's degree in International Relations Management (2003). She has a PhD in philology since 2006 and a graduate (2017) of the Faculty of Psychology and Educational Sciences, UBB. She works as a Clinical Psychologist and Counsel for Personal Development at the FSPAC Career Center. The current research activity focuses on topics in the fields: communication sciences, media psychology, digitalization and the human psyche, consumer behavior, contemporary mentalities.
