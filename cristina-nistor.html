Conf. dr. Cristina Nistor

Associate Professor Ph.D.

Cristina Nistor has been a faculty member since 2001 and she has been coordinating the English Journalism Program since 2010. In the meantime, she also conducted a six-year professional activity as Spokesperson of Babeș-Bolyai University. She completed her PhD with a thesis on Media Coverage of Crisis, including a visiting research program at New York University and Columbia University in New York, US. She has a M.A. in Social Communication and Public Relations and a B.A. in Journalism and Communication. She obtained her Habilitation in 2017 with the thesis Communicating with the Media. Professional and Academic Practices in Journalism. Her courses and research focus on European Communication, Crisis Communication, Media Relations in the Digital Age, Radio Journalism and Media Systems and Industries.
