Lect. dr. Viorel Nistor

Lecturer Ph.D.

Viorel Nistor has 15 years of journalistic experience, an entrepreneurial practice of six years, and since 2005 he has been a teacher at FSPAC, then associate professor. A double bachelor, from UT and UBB, he has a doctorate in literary sciences, with a topic about the political novel. He has written various scientific articles (literary and journalistic) but also collective and personal volumes. The research focus is on new forms of specialized journalism (investigative, data, political, etc.), and topics such as the media system crisis, media entrepreneurship, crowdsourcing and journalistic crowdfunding.
