Asist. cercet. drd. Flavia Țăran

Research assistant Ph.D.c

Flavia Țăran holds as BA in Journalism and a MA in Media Communication, both from the Department of Journalism. She continues her studies here, currently elaborating her PhD thesis on internet memes and viral phenomena, while also conducting research and teaching workshops and seminars on digital data analysis, web design, content management systems and data journalism.
