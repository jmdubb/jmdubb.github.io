Lect. dr. Oana Ometa

Lecturer Ph.D.

Oana Ometa graduated Journalism in 2007. She is part of the permanent faculty at the Journalism and Digital Media Department since 2018. She holds a Ph.D. in History with a thesis on the condition of the journalists in the communist era, an oral history research. Her research interests include history, oral history, cultural journalism, public journalism/citizen journalism, media anthropology. She still collaborates with cultural journals and online publications.
