Lect. dr. Péter Árpád

Lecturer Ph.D.

Péter Árpád graduated in 2000, at the Theoretical High School "Orbán Balázs" from Cristuru-Secuiesc. In 2004 he graduated from the Faculty of Letters (BBU), specializing in Hungarian-French. In 2013 he wrote his PhD thesis at FSPAC (BBU), in the theory of interactive digital media, with the coordination of Prof. Cseke Péter. Since 2006 he is a collaborator, and since 2017 lecturer of FSPAC, at the Department of Journalism and Digital Media, where he teaches disciplines related to design and postmodern media culture. The main research areas are media in the virtual space, contemporary films, popular culture in the media, contemporary media consumption, postmodern myths.
