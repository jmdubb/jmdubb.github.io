Lect. dr. Rareș Beuran

Lecturer Ph.D.

Rareș Beuran has been a faculty member since 2006, and he has founded the Media Studio of the Journalism and Digital Media Department which includes students’ projects UBB RADIO ONLINE and UBB TV ONLINE. He has both an academic and professional experience in journalism, as he worked as a photojournalist and TV news-reporter for several years in the past and subsequently had a significant experience as project manager in media related programs. He completed his PhD with a thesis focusing on Media and Diversity and he has a M.A. in Communication Sciences. His courses and research focus on Photojournalism in the Digital Age, Visual Grammar of TV News, Intercultural Communication in Media and Digital Innovations in European Reporting.
