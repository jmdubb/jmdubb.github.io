Lect. dr. Constantin Trofin

Lecturer Ph.D.

Active in Television since 1991: TV Journalist and anchor, general producer TVR Cluj, owner of a private TV production company, programming director NCN TV, advisor for the General Manager of the National Television of the Republic of Moldova, sead of the Special Programs Department of TVR, executive Producer LookTV. TV Shows (produced and hosted): Various news programs for TVR Cluj as a reporter, producer and anchor, Ştirile de sâmbăta asta – TVR1 (1998–2002), Canalul de știri – matinal Antena 1 (2003-2006), position 1-2 in national ratings (national urban commercial public), Dis de seară – TVR1 (2006–2007), La Mustață –TVR2 (2011–2012). 
Awards: Three times winner of the Prize of the APTR – Bucharest (Romanian TV Professionals Association ); Mediafest Prize for Best TV Entertainment – Mediafest Bucharest; The TeleVest Trophy – TeleVest Timisoara; Prize for Best TV Host – Televest – Timisoara.
