Conf. dr. Andreea Mogoș

Associate Professor Ph.D.

Andreea Mogoș is part of the faculty at the Journalism and Digital Media Department since 1998. She has a BA in Journalism and a BA in Sociology. She earned a PhD in Sociology at Babes-Bolyai University and a PhD in Information and Communication Sciences at Université Paris 8 Vincennes – Saint-Denis with a thesis on the media representations of the Romanians in the French daily newspapers. She obtained her Habilitation in 2016, with the thesis Traditional and new media representations. From a socially constructed reality to a filtered and quantified reality. Her research interests include media representations and the social construction of reality, image and visualisation analysis, media genres and their transformation.
