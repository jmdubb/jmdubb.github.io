Lect. dr. Győrffy Gábor

Lecturer Ph.D.

Győrffy Gábor obtained his master’s degree at the Faculty of Physics, Babeș-Bolyai University of Cluj-Napoca in 1997, obtaining afterwards, in 2000, the certification of teaching Hungarian and English languages at the Faculty of Arts. He holds a Ph.D. in Humanities with a thesis on the censorship and propaganda in the Romania in the period of communist dictatorship. His research interests include history of media and comparative analysis of media structures.
