Conf. dr. Tibori-Szabó Zoltán

Associate Professor Ph.D.

Zoltán Tibori-Szabó (PhD) is associate professor of journalism and political communication at the Journalism and Digital Media Department, teaching among other courses Investigative Reporting, Political Journalism, Diplomacy, and Journalism in Conflict Zones. His research areas are related to the transformations of the public sphere, media history, and Holocaust studies. Author of books and studies dealing with the Holocaust in Hungary and Transylvania, he is also director of the Institute for Holocaust and Genocide Studies and of the Media Systems and Languages Research Center of our college.
