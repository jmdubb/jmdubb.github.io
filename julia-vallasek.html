Conf. dr. Júlia Réka Vallasek

Associate Professor Ph.D.

Júlia Réka Vallasek is part of th faculty at the Journalism Department since 2000. She graduated at the Faculty of Letters (Hungarian-English), Babeș- Bolyai University and earned MA degree on the field of Literature and Society in 1999. She holds a PhD in History of literature earned at the University of Debrecen (Hungary). Her research interests include: cultural journalism, history of the press, construction of alterity and identity. Contributor of several cultural and litarary reviews in Romania and abroad, literary translator and critic.
